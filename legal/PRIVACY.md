# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in the "reading-time" Chrome extension.

## 1. Data Collection
The "reading-time" extension **does not collect, store, or transmit** any personal data. All processing of web page content to calculate reading time happens locally on your device.

## 2. External Communication
The extension **does not communicate with any external servers**. There is no background tracking or data reporting.

## 3. Local Storage
The extension uses `chrome.storage.local` to store your preferences (such as reading speed). This data remains on your device and is not shared.

## 4. Third-Party Access
Since we do not collect any data, no third parties have access to your information.

## 5. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer.
